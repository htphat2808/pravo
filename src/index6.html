Meet Our Doctors

Bryce J. Bott, DMD

Pediatric Dentist

I grew up in Brigham City, Utah, where, as a youth and avid scouter, I was inspired to become a dentist by my Scoutmaster. I set my sights on dentistry and began preparing for this career as early as high school and continued on to Utah State University studying biology, chemistry, and public health. I received my Doctor of Dental Medicine from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas School of Dental Medicine, and pediatric specialty training at the University of Nebraska Medical Center and the Children’s Hospital & Medical Center.

I love being a pediatric dentist! It is the perfect blend of art, science and fun! It allows me to work with the most kind, happy and entertaining population.

✦ STORY

I believe it is very important for all children to have access to quality dental care and to feel empowered with the knowledge and resources to have a healthy smile for life. A positive dental experience combines the very best care tailored to each child’s unique personality and needs with a relationship built on trust and communication.

My goal as a pediatric dentist is to provide a dental home and gentle care for infants, children, adolescents and patients with special needs.
