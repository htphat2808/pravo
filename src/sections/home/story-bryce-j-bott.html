✦ STORY

I believe it is very important for all children to have access to quality dental care and to feel empowered with the knowledge and resources to have a healthy smile for life. A positive dental experience combines the very best care tailored to each child’s unique personality and needs with a relationship built on trust and communication.

My goal as a pediatric dentist is to provide a dental home and gentle care for infants, children, adolescents and patients with special needs.
